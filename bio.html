Who am I?

Currently located in London, I am a Brazilian-Finn who is passionate about blockchain technology and social impact initiatives. I am the co-founder of the largest open blockchain education platform in Brazil, BlockchainBH, and have worked as an educator, developer and lecturer in the field. Coming from a Business Management background, I am currently an Ethereum developer and have some additional experience with web and backend. As of right now, I hold a position as a Visiting Scholar at IGTI, where I helped develop the first blockchain-focused MBA in Brazil, as well as work as a Project Lead at London Blockchain Labs. Outside of tech, my interests include Writing, Design, Photography, Hiking and Calisthenics. I speak four languages, tell stories like a Brazilian, and withstand cold like a Finn. I am a go-getter who learned how to code, design, do financial accounting, pitch and lead in order to be able to get projects off the ground with minimal help.
